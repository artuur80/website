Brucon Student CTF is a capture the flag event organized by Howest to promote Brucon. Events like these require a team. In our team was Kerim Popelier, Brend Lambert, Bert Van Mulders, Elias Vandewalle, Valmir Mulaj en me Mattijs Step. This event was about solving puzzles in different area's. These included all sorts of IT topics such as Malware analysis and forensic analysis. Not only were their challenges on our computer but their were also challenges like lockpicking and building LEGO. This was a fun day full of exciting stuff to do.
