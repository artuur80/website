Cyber Tuesday is an initiative of Howest that gives information about a Cybersecurity topic. This evening was about IC4 or Industrial Control & Communication Competence Center. This research team taught us the importance of security in Industrial security. IC4 team consist of Kurt Callewaert, Johannes Cottyn, Tijl Deneut, Tinus Umans and Hendrik Derre. This session spread awareness of Industrial systems security flaws. This event was part of our study program and was our first introduction into Industrial security. The evening was really good they even had a small factory with them. On that setup they showed the possible security flaws. Despite us not knowing much of Industrial systems I learned a lot. I would recommend this.
