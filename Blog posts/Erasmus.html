For the second semester of my studies I decided to to an internship abroad. After discussing with our international coach and with the university of Sankt Pölten I was ready to head to Austria. My internship was about a new Steganography technique that would use BitTorrent to make it more efficient. Here in Sankt Pölten I was not the only study that came to do their Erasmus. We were in total around 20 people coming from all over the world: Ecuador, South-Africa, Russia, Azerbaijan, Spain, Italy, Portugal, France, UK and Belgium. In the week before their studies we had a lot of activities to get to know each other better. After a month of doing my internship COVID-19 changed the erasmus. I had to work from my apartment instead of the university. All travel had been cancelled. During this period I made a deeper connection with a few erasmus students. Every other evening we would play boardgames to keep ourselves entertained. For photo's click me
