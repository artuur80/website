For the international project I chose to go to India. This project was really unforgettable experience. Our project was separated into two parts. The first part was a visiting trip around India and then the second week was the actual project. Our first week consisted of us visiting the different buildings and monuments of India. This included Taj Mahal, Amber Palace, Monkey temple, border with Pakistan, the mountains in the North and more. The second week we were divided into groups. Each group had their own project that they worked on. My group included Kashish, Jayand, Vishal, Keshav, Alexander and me. We worked on a project for detecting and identifying people so that they can be used in an attendance program. That way they don't need to say the names they just need a camera and our little program. After the days of the project we went with our group to do all sort of stuff in the evening.
