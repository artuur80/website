Job Description: Business Analyst

Your job is to analyse data and tell the business how to move forward.

This will entail that you sift through data and make choices.

We expect you to have a great insight in some matters. The main job you will have to do is making reports. You will also have to do a lot of communication with your team

At the end of the day it will be a meeting that summarize what you have done during the day

We search someone who can make good choices and is stubborn. We search for a goodhearted person who has the best interests in the company. We offer you a great workplace with a team that supports you. We are a steady company with medium amount of workers for us.

We would like for you to have a medium IT related background. We ourselves will guide you through the financial part.

You will need to have some social capabilities. You need to be able to speak Dutch as well as English for possible clients and team members

We offer a test to see if you fit in our business and if you have this job in you.
